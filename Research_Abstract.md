# Research Abstract
## Soil Health

I am investigating farming practices and the effects of those practices on soil health. In my research, I will compare different ways to improve soil health. Another aspect of research will be how different soil types are more or less affected by farming practices. I will also research the relationship of soil and microorganisms.
